Discover the joy of dancing with our beginner-friendly classes. Our experienced instructors guide you through the fundamentals of the International Ballroom Standard and Latin dances like the waltz, foxtrot, cha-cha, rumba, and more - in a supportive, welcoming environment. You'll learn proper posture, basic steps, and rhythmic patterns while developing coordination and confidence on the dance floor.
No prior experience or partner is necessary - we'll pair you up during class. Whether you're preparing for a special event or simply want to try something new, our classes offer a fun way to stay active, meet new people, and master the timeless art of ballroom dance. Join us and take your first steps towards becoming a graceful, skilled dancer.
